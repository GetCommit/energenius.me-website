Solar Decision Making Tools

  • Jobs and Economic Development Impacts (JEDI) estimates the economic impacts of constructing and operating photovoltaic power generation at the local and state levels.
  • PVWatts calculates electrical energy produced by a grid-connected photovoltaic (PV) system.
  • System Advisor Model (SAM) is a comprehensive solar technology systems analysis model.
